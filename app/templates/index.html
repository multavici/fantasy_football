Rules of the game

Team result

  • Team Win: 3 pts
  • Team Draw: 1 pt
  • Team Loss: 0 pt

* In the knock-out stages, the result after extra time counts

Individual performance

  • Points of the player on whoscored.com
  • Man of the Match on whoscored.com: 5 pts

Goals

  • Attacker/midfielder/defender: 5 pts
  • Goal keeper: 10 pts

Assists

  • 3 pts

Clean sheets

  • Goal keeper: 5 pts
  • Defender: 3 pts
  • Attacker/midfielder: 1 pt

* for players who played a minimum of 60 minutes

Penalties

  • Scored penalty: 5 pts
  • Missed penalty: -5 pts
  • Goal keeper who saves penalty: 5 pts

* also counts for penalties during shoot-out in knock-out phase

Cards

  • Yellow card: -1 pt
  • Red card: -3 pts

* 2nd yellow card = red card = -3 pts

Own goals

  • -3 pts
